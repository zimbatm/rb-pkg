This is the current conventions used by setup.rb

PackageTop/
    setup.rb
    lib/
        (ruby scripts)
    ext/
        (ruby extentions)
    bin/
        (commands)
    data/
        (data files)
    conf/
        (configuration files)
    man/
        (manual pages)
    test/
        (tests)
